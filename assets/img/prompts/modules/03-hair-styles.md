# Module 03 — Hair Styles（发型选项）

> 每段前 `HAIR-X` 是代号，引用时一字不差。

---

## HAIR-A · High Ponytail（高马尾 — 都市 / 赛事）

```
Long black hair in a loose high ponytail swept to one side.
```

字符：~55

---

## HAIR-B · Low Messy Bun（低髻 — 早晨厨房 / 私域）

```
A few strands of long black hair falling from a low messy bun to frame her face.
```

字符：~80

---

## HAIR-C · Long Loose（长直发散落 — 雨夜窗边 / 卧室）

```
Long straight black hair falling loosely past her shoulders, slightly windswept.
```

字符：~70

---

## HAIR-D · Half-up Half-down（半扎 — 书房 / 茶室）

```
Long black hair half-up half-down, secured with a small ivory claw clip.
```

字符：~70

---

## HAIR-E · Side Braid（侧编辫 — 古街 / 庭院）

```
Long black hair in a loose single braid draped over one shoulder.
```

字符：~55

---

## 搭配建议

| 场景 | 推荐 HAIR |
|------|-----------|
| 赛事 / 都市 | A |
| 早晨厨房 / 阳台 | B |
| 雨夜 / 卧室 | C |
| 书房 / 沙发 | D |
| 茶室 / 古街 / 庭院 | E |
