# Module 02 — Outfits（服装选项）

> 每段前 `OUTFIT-X` 是代号，引用时一字不差。

---

## OUTFIT-A · Black Halter（黑底深 V — 赛事 / 都市感）

```
She wears a sleek fitted deep-V halter-neck top in all-black matte stretch fabric, the halter straps revealing her bare back and shoulders, paired with high-waisted wide-leg black trousers. Tinted sunglasses pushed up on her head, small gold ear studs.
```

字符：~210 · 偏冷 / 都市 / 夜晚赛事

---

## OUTFIT-B · Cream Linen Apron（米白围裙 — 早晨厨房 / 私域）

```
She wears a soft cream linen apron over a thin ivory cotton camisole, apron ties loose behind her waist.
```

字符：~110 · 偏暖 / 私域 / 早晨

---

## OUTFIT-C · Off-shoulder Slip（露肩吊带 — 卧室 / 雨夜窗边）

```
She wears a thin silk ivory slip dress with delicate spaghetti straps, one strap fallen off her shoulder.
```

字符：~100 · 偏柔 / 卧室 / 雨夜

---

## OUTFIT-D · Cashmere Knit（羊毛开衫 — 书房 / 沙发）

```
She wears an oversized cream cashmere cardigan over a thin white tee, sleeves pushed up past her wrists, the cardigan slipping off one shoulder.
```

字符：~120 · 偏暖 / 慵懒 / 室内

---

## OUTFIT-E · Hanfu-Modern（新中式 — 茶室 / 古街）

```
She wears a soft ivory qipao-inspired top with mandarin collar and side slits, paired with wide-leg linen trousers in muted sage green.
```

字符：~125 · 偏古 / 茶室 / 庭院
