# Module 04 — Environments（场景选项）

> 每段前 `ENV-X` 是代号，引用时一字不差。
> 每段包含：场景 + 远景 + 前景 + 光线四件套。

---

## ENV-A · Stadium Spectator（赛事观众）

```
She stands at a railing leaning toward the action. Behind her, a distant out-of-focus background with bold fictional blue and orange colors, something fast moving streaking past. Soft blurred foreground under her hands. Bright afternoon sunlight, sharp clean exposure.
```

字符：~250 · 户外 / 赛事 / 强光

---

## ENV-B · Morning Kitchen（早晨厨房）

```
She stands at a small vintage gas stove in a sunlit morning kitchen — one hand stirring a claypot of congee with a long wooden spoon, the other hand lifting the wooden lid slightly. Behind her, a tall window overlooks a quiet residential courtyard, green potted plants on the sill, soft steam rising from the pot. Soft blurred foreground of ceramic mugs and a small bunch of rosemary in a glass jar on the counter. Bright golden morning sunlight streaming through the window, warm exposure, soft shadows.
```

字符：~480 · 私域 / 早晨 / 暖光

---

## ENV-C · Rainy Window（雨夜窗边）

```
She stands by a tall window at night, one hand lightly resting on the cool glass, watching the rain streak down. Behind her, a softly lit bedroom with a single warm lamp. Soft blurred foreground of a ceramic teacup on the windowsill. Cool blue-gray night light from the rain, mixed with warm amber lamp glow inside. Low ISO, gentle grain.
```

字符：~330 · 卧室 / 雨夜 / 冷暖混光

---

## ENV-D · Reading Room（书房沙发）

```
She sits cross-legged on a deep linen sofa, leaning forward with a hardcover book resting on her knees, mid-turn of a page. Behind her, floor-to-ceiling bookshelves packed with books, a single warm floor lamp in the corner. Soft blurred foreground of a half-read teacup and reading glasses on a side table. Late afternoon golden light through tall windows, warm soft shadows.
```

字符：~340 · 室内 / 下午 / 暖光

---

## ENV-E · Tea Courtyard（茶室庭院）

```
She stands at an old stone table in a quiet courtyard, both hands resting on the rim of a celadon tea set, a curl of steam rising from the pot. Behind her, bamboo, a wooden lattice window, and a single stone lantern. Soft blurred foreground of fallen ginkgo leaves on the stone path. Soft diffused overcast daylight, no harsh shadows, gentle warmth.
```

字符：~330 · 庭院 / 阴天柔光
